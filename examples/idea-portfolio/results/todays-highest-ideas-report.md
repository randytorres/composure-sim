### Forge Engine

Highest ideas: FORGE GAME, FORGE APPSTORE FACTORY, FORGE GAME FEEL BENCHMARK, ROVIQ FORGE KIT, FORGE EDITOR DESKTOP.

Forge is the deepest technical edge. The docs describe a C++20 runtime, Swift gameplay/editor layer, direct Metal renderer, audio/haptic runtime hooks, replay/debug hooks, Apple device loop, editor, AI control plane, patch review, services, and a premium-feedback roadmap focused on hit confirm, whiff, dodge, hurt, windup, ambience, music, thermal degradation, runtime residency, LOD, batching, and hardware truth.

Best first slice: public "Forge Game Feel Benchmark" plus Saint Caliber playable slice. The benchmark proves native feel; the slice proves product value.

### The Escape / AGON

Highest ideas: AGON PROOF FEED, AGON COUNCIL API, AGON ORDER, AGON ATLAS, AGON FATHERS, CAPTAIN OS.

AGON is unusually clear as a product system: the product is the season, the app is the venue. Season 1 is 100 men, 10 squads, 30 days, $99, Mission Card, Captain, Proof Feed, Locker Room, Scoreboard, Day-30 Pentathlon, and founder public practice. The sharpest architectural detail is the Captain/Council split: Captain orchestrates the squad; Council answers the AAR.

Best first slice: Proof Feed plus Captain proof-check. Council API is more beautiful, but Proof Feed creates the receipts that make the league real.

### Roviq

Highest ideas: ROVIQ STUDIO STACK, THE ROVIQ UNIVERSE, ROVIQ FORGE KIT, Roviq Universe.

Roviq's actual edge is the house style: named people with attributes, mechanics-as-morality, multi-generational time, cultural specificity, AI-cinematic atmosphere, hidden information, and permanent death. The studio stack turns scattered projects into one reusable production system.

Best first slice: Roviq Forge Kit. It keeps PARAGON and REALM from becoming two separate engines wearing similar clothes.
